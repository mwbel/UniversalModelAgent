# \$36.8. RADIATION REACTION IN SLOW-MOTION SOURCES\*

The conservation laws discussed in Box 19.1 and derived in $\ S 2 0 . 5$ guarantee that the source must lose energy and angular momentum at the same rate as the gravita-tional waves carry them off.
